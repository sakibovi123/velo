

## Fin AI Agent (their flagship autonomous AI)
- Custom LLM architecture trained specifically on customer service interactions, with a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) system
- Learns from public and private knowledge sources — Help Center articles, internal docs, PDFs, webpages
- Audience targeting so Fin surfaces content based on plan, location, or brand
- Customizable personality — tone of voice (professional, friendly, humorous) and answer length controls
- Fin Guidance — custom instructions to enforce support policies and communication style
- Suggestions feature that recommends content improvements based on conversations Fin couldn't resolve
- Fin Tasks — automation of multi-step processes (e.g., canceling orders, processing refunds)
- Procedures — a document-style editor with code and data connectors for complex queries
- Knowledge Snippets for adding custom Q&A directly into the system
- Data connectors to real-time systems via APIs (Shopify, Salesforce, etc.), including MCP connector support
- Multi-channel deployment across Messenger, WhatsApp, SMS, email, Slack, Discord, social, and voice (Fin Voice for phone calls)
- Fin CSAT (conversation rating collection)
- Auto-close of pending conversations and configurable handover to human agents

## Fin AI Copilot (agent assistance)
- AI assistant for every agent that pulls context from past conversations, surfaces instant answers from the knowledge base, and drafts replies
- Conversation summarization, reply personalization, and auto-completion of simple actions
- Access to copilot-only sources like Notion and Confluence

## Inbox / Helpdesk
- Omnichannel shared inbox unifying chat, email, phone, WhatsApp, SMS, and social
- One-click conversation-to-ticket conversion with auto-categorization, prioritization, and routing
- Side conversations (with export, real-time updates, direct email links)
- SLA management with sortable/filterable metrics (First Reply Time, Next Reply Time, Time to Close, Time to Resolution) and at-a-glance timers
- Editable internal notes with "edited" indicators and permissions
- Balanced assignment with auto-reassignment for away teammates
- Snooze, merge conversations & tickets (in beta)
- Conversation tags, filters, and custom ticket states
- AI Inbox Translations with customizable glossary for specific terms
- Pinnable inboxes and customizable reports menu

## Messenger (customer-facing widget)
- Spaces — Home, Messages, and Help tabs (with in-Messenger Help Center that can sync with Zendesk or Salesforce articles)
- Configurable Messenger Launcher, CTAs, privacy policy notices, and per-domain restrictions
- JWT-based user authentication to prevent impersonation and session theft
- Attachment controls, sound preferences, and branding customization
- Web, iOS, and Android SDKs
